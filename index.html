About Me

My name is Howie Reith, software engineer, writer, and singer.

I recently completed General Assembly's Web Development Immersive in which I developed the projects you will find on this page.

I earned my college degree in voice performance before pursuing a career in finance and accounting. There I discovered a love for automating processes which led me to explore options in coding and tech. General Assembly was the training program I decided to pursue.
